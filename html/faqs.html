About StyleLab

Q: From where do you operate?
A: We are based in Toronto, Canada.
Q: Where are your products made?
A: StyleLab products are all proudly made in Canada.
Q: Where can we reach you?
A: For exchanges and other concerns about your order, please email us at customerservice@stylelab.com. For other inquiries, you may email us at info@stylelab.com or call us at +1 (437) 123-4567.

Orders & Shipping

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: We are looking into being able to ship internationally but for now, we only ship within Canada.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept debit and credit card payments from major providers and Paypal.
Q: Do you accept bank transfers?
A: At the moment, we do not.
Q: After submitting an order, when do I hear back?
A: We will get back to you through the email associated with your account within 24 hours with the total amount of your order (including taxes and shipping fee). You will then have 24 hours to respond to confirm your order before we push through with the shipment.
Q: How long does the shipment take?
A: We ship out confirmed items every Saturday and depending on where you are in Canada, it may take 3 to 14 days before you receive your order.

Returns, Exchanges, & Refunds

Q: Do you accept returns and/or exchanges?
A: We allow size exchanges if stocks are available. Just let us know within a week of receiving your order and make sure that the tags are still on. Otherwise, all sales are final.
Q: Do you allow refunds?
A: No, we do not.
